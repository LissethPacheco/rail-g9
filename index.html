Railway as a Platform (PaaS) Lis

Railway is a modern platform-as-a-service (PaaS) that enables simple application deployment, integrates seamlessly with GitHub, and automates your workflow. It's ideal for developers who want to focus on code without worrying about infrastructure.
Source: docs.railway.com/overview/about-railway

What is Railway?

Railway lets you build, deploy, and scale applications from a unified platform. You can connect your GitHub repository and Railway will manage the entire deployment lifecycle.
Read more: About Railway

Railway Philosophy

Railway aims to eliminate developer friction by automating repetitive tasks and empowering fast iteration. It emphasizes simplicity, collaboration, and seamless scalability.
Source: docs.railway.com/maturity/philosophy

Railway Pricing Plans

Railway offers free and paid tiers tailored to different developer needs. The free plan is great for personal projects, while paid plans scale with professional use.
View pricing: Pricing Plans

GitHub Actions Integration

You can automate your deployments with GitHub Actions. This means every time you push to your repository, Railway will build and deploy your app automatically.
Learn more: GitHub Actions with Railway
